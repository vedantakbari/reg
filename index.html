Server Rules

To maintain a friendly and safe environment, we enforce the following rules

  • Respect all members at all times. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
  • No spamming or excessive self-promotion in any channels.
  • Keep discussions in appropriate channels according to the topic.
  • No sharing links to external websites without moderator approval.
  • No sharing or requesting exploits that violate Roblox Terms of Service.
  • Payment for services only after completion to prevent scams.
  • No blank, inappropriate, or offensive nicknames or profile pictures.
  • No sharing personal information of yourself or others.
  • Report any issues or rule violations to moderators through appropriate channels.
  • All members must follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
